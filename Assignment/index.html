About ICU Zambia

A leading private university dedicated to excellence in information and communication education.

2009 Established
HEA Accredited

Leading the Digital Revolution in Zambia

Information and Communication University (ICU Zambia) is a private higher education institution located in Lusaka, Zambia. Founded in 2009, ICU Zambia is officially recognised by the Ministry of Education and the Higher Education Authority (HEA).

We are dedicated to providing quality, industry-aligned education in information and communication technologies, equipping graduates for leadership in Zambia's digital economy.

  • Fully accredited by the Higher Education Authority (HEA) of Zambia
  • Industry-partnered curriculum updated annually
  • State-of-the-art computer labs and high-speed campus network
  • 95% graduate employment rate within 6 months of completion
