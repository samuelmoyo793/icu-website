Who We Are

About ICU Zambia

A leading private university dedicated to excellence in information and communication education.

2009 Established
HEA Accredited

Shaping Minds. Transforming Lives.

Information and Communications University (ICU) is built on the principle that "education should inspire invention, not just usage." Its legacy traces to the original ICU founded in Daejeon, South Korea in 1998 — a research-focused ICT institution that merged with KAIST in 2009, after which dedicated faculty established ICU Zambia to carry that spirit forward in Africa.

Located off Shantumbu Road, Kafue, ICU Zambia offers accredited programmes in Information & Communications Technology, Business, Education, Engineering, and Humanities — designed to prepare graduates for a knowledge-driven world.

  • Accredited by the Higher Education Authority (HEA) of Zambia
  • Four Schools: Humanities, Business, Education & Engineering
  • Partnerships with KAIST, ZRDC, Multiresearch & Russian Language Centre
  • Digital library access via library.icuzambia.net
